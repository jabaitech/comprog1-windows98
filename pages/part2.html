Selection Criteria - Will it be eye catching or not?


  • -- Motivation and Vision --
    Motivation and vision are vital in visual media like films, advertisements, and documentaries, as they inspire audiences, drive narratives, and shape the overall impact of a visual story.
  • -- Relevance and Currency --
    In visual media, current and appropriate content to its target audience and purpose is referred to as relevance and currency.
  • -- Engaging and Eye-Catching --
    In order to successfully capture consumers' attention and deliver messages, visual content is essential.
  • -- Understandable and Cohesive Design --
    Using harmonious colors, forms, textures, and typography, visual media designers create a unified and clear visual language that improves content engagement and comprehension.
